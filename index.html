Research Projects

PI-1424 - An infraestructure for primary data access with semantic in digital repositories

The current needs for interoperability and vocabulary uniformity have led to rethinking the classical models of knowledge organization in digital repositories.In this context the semantic searches begin to be important. That is why this project raises the definition of an infrastructure to integrate through the use of semantic web technologies the information with other Digital Repositories, to accomplish discovery of knowledge that is implicit in the data and finally the creation of prototypes to demonstrate its exploitation.

PGI-UNS (24/N038) Semantic Interoperability: applications for the Web and E-Government

This project aims to investigate and develop methodologies and tools that favor the semantic interoperability of information and services on the Web, based on the latest advances in the area of knowledge representation languages, ontologies and conceptual modeling. In addition, its application in the area of electronic government will be promoted where the need for integration is more urgent.
